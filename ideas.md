## Selected Design: Dark Luxury (Response 2)

Playfair Display + Inter. Deep charcoal (#0A0A0A) background. Muted gold (#C9A84C) accents. Asymmetric editorial grid. This matches the existing site's black/gold aesthetic while elevating it to institutional-grade quality. The live data elements (BTC price, factor count) will be integrated tastefully — data as credibility, not noise.
